Tokyo City

As the capitol of Japan, Tokyo is a great and common place for tours!

Traditional Kyoto Cusine Walking Tour

Stroll through Nishiki Market, buying all the produce you need, and then strolling through the Horin Sake Museum, this tour ends at a traditional teahouse for a cooking class using the very ingredients you bought earlier!

Ghibli Anime Museum

Not a tour specifically, however it's a prime tourist location for any and all fans of Studio Ghibli movies! (It's quite popular, so make sure to purchase tickets in advance!!)
