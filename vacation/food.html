Food & Dining

Sushi

Japan is well-known for its sushi - being an island nation they're surrounded by fish, some of which aren't seen anywhere else in the world,

Sukiyabashi Jiro Roppongi

The first restaurant in Japan to achieve 3 Michelin stars, this sushi restaurant is so popular it's almost impossible to get a seat! If you're going for this place, make sure to book plenty in advance!

Ramen

Another popular food in Japan is Ramen, with various types of noodles (typically alkaline wheat noodles) and toppings such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, bamboo, eggs, and more!

Ichiran Ramen

Ichiran Ramen is a ramen restaraunt chain in Japan built specifically for introverts and people with social anxiety! Everything is ordered and delivered without any interaction with the server, perfect for a break from people.
