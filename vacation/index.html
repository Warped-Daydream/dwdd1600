An Introduction to Japan

Planning a trip to Japan? You're in the right place! This website includes all the need-to-know information for the perfect vacation!

Japan is rich in culture and a prime travel location for many people, but even then it's full of small secrets that most may not be aware of: did you know there's an island full of rabbits? A train with cats on it? What about the ramen place specifically for introverts and those with social anxiety? On this website, we explore all that and more so you don't miss anything that could pique your interest on your vacation!
