Ice

Category Rules

As many of the sub elements can be arranged to be under just about any of the main for (more or less) I've come up with some baseline rules as to what elements can go in this category:

  1. If it is related to ice, water, or is associated with cold, it's ice
  2. If it would best be considered an extension of water (eg, very fluid things), it's ice
  3. If it requires other of the main 4 elements, is closer related to other main 4 elements, or has ice as a sub of its own abilities, it's not ice.

short and sweet, I know, but it works quite well.

Elements and Elemental Masters Under Ice

Ice
The elemental power of creating and manipulating cold/ice. Weilded during the Serpentine War by Hibernus, who intentionally passed it onto Zane Here's more info on Zane!
Shadow
The elemental power of turning into/manipulating/teleporting through shadows. Not seen during the Serptentine War, but currently weilded by Shade
Water
The elemental power of controlling water. Weilded during the Serpentine War by Maya, now weilded by her daughter, Nya
Form
The elemental power of shapeshifting. Not seen during the Serpentine War, but currently weilded by Chamille
