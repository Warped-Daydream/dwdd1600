Other

Category Rules

In short, this is the category for any abilities that can't easily go under the other categories.

Elements and Elemental Masters Under This Category

Golden Power
The ultimate power of light and creation. First weilded by the First Spinjitzu master, then his grandson Lloyd Garmadon. The Overlord temporarily weilds this power as well, under stolen pretenses. Lloyd divided it shortly afterwards.
Darkness
The ultimate power of darkness and destruction. Weilded by the Overlord.
Creation
The ability to create. Weilded by Wu.
Destruction
The ability to destroy. Weilded by Garmadon
Amber
The ability to copy and use other elemental abilities. Weilded by Skylor.
Time
The ability to stop, slow, speed up, and reverse time. Weilded by time twins Krux and Acronix during the serpentine wars, now in special blades as the two attempted to use their powers for evil.
Green
A very, very weakened version of the Golden Power, also called Energy. Currently weilded by Lloyd Garmadon.
Brown
Not actually an element, but a temporarily granted ability. It creates a brown flame with an unknown effect. Dareth is the only known character to weild it.
Mailman
Not an element, but basically is because how else do you explain a man being able to track down a flying boat, catch up to it by pedaling a winged bike, all to deliver the mail. Weilded by the Mailman
