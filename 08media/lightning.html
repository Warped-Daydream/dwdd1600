Lightning

Category Rules

As many of the sub elements can be arranged to be under just about any of the main for (more or less) I've come up with some baseline rules as to what elements can go in this category:

  1. If it is related to electricity, the sky, or is associated with either, it's lightning
  2. If it requires other of the main 4 elements, is closer related to other main 4 elements, or has lightning as a sub of its own abilities, it's not lightning.

short and sweet, I know, but it works quite well.

Elements and Elemental Masters Under Lightning

Lightning
The element of generating (and controlling, to an extent) lightning/electricity. Weilded by an unknown woman during the Serpentine War, who is the mother of the current weilder, Jay
Mind
The element of reading and influencing other's minds. Not seen during the Serpentine War, but currently weilded by a man named Neuro
Speed
The element of being... really fast. Not seen during the Serpentine War, but currently weilded by a man named Griffin Turner
Wind
The element of controlling the air. Not seen during the Serpentine War, and current weilder is unknown as previous weilder, Morro, died.
Sound
The element of manipulating and reading sound (includes echolocation!) Weilded by an unnamed man during the Serpentine War, and currently weilded by Jacob Pevsner.
