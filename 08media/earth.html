Earth

Category Rules

As many of the sub elements can be arranged to be under just about any of the main for (more or less) I've come up with some baseline rules as to what elements can go in this category:

  1. If it is related to dirt, rocks, or manipulating the land/planet beneath our feet, it's earth
  2. If it's related to plants, it's earth
  3. If it requires other of the main 4 elements, is closer related to other main 4 elements, or has earth as a sub of its own abilities, it's not earth.

short and sweet, I know, but it works quite well.

Elements and Elemental Masters Under Earth

Earth
The elemental power of controlling rocks and dirt. Weilded in the Serpentine War by an unnamed man, who passed it onto his grandson, Cole.
Nature
The elemental power of controlling plants. Not seen during the Serpentine War, but is currently weilded by Bolobo
Gravity
The elemental power of controlling gravity (in small amounts.) Weilded in the Serpentine War by a man named Levi, who passed it on to his descendent, Gravis
Metal
The elemental power of turning one's own body into metal. Not seen during the Serpentine War, but is currently weilded by Karlof
Poison
The elemental power of controlling poisons. Not seen during the Serpentine War, but is currently weilded by Tox
